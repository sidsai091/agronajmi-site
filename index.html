About Us

Cultivating Growth Through Modern Agriculture

NAJMI AGRICULTURE is a Bumiputera company registered under the Registration of Businesses Act 1956 on 25 April 2023, specializing in supplying and installing agricultural goods, especially fertigation systems. We serve government agencies and public clients with quality products, installation, and advisory services, blending traditional and modern farming techniques.

Our Expertise

Company Background

Discover our diverse range of services and expertise across multiple sectors.

We provide comprehensive construction services for agricultural infrastructure, including greenhouses, farm buildings, storage facilities, and irrigation infrastructure. Our team ensures quality construction that meets industry standards and supports sustainable farming operations.

  • Greenhouse construction
  • Farm building development
  • Storage facility construction
  • Infrastructure development

Our specialty lies in fertigation systems that combine irrigation with fertilization for optimal plant nutrition. We design, supply, and install advanced fertigation solutions that maximize crop yield while minimizing resource waste.

  • Automated fertigation systems
  • Nutrient management solutions
  • Precision dosing equipment
  • System maintenance & support

We specialize in agricultural piping systems for water distribution, irrigation networks, and drainage solutions. Our piping installations are designed for durability, efficiency, and easy maintenance.

  • Water distribution networks
  • Drip irrigation piping
  • Drainage systems
  • HDPE & PVC installations

Our core expertise in agriculture covers modern farming techniques, crop management, and sustainable practices. We provide consultation and implementation services to help farmers achieve optimal yields while preserving the environment.

  • Crop management consulting
  • Sustainable farming practices
  • Agricultural technology integration
  • Farm planning & development

We supply high-quality agricultural products, equipment, and materials to support farming operations. Our supply chain ensures timely delivery of essential items from trusted manufacturers and suppliers.

  • Agricultural equipment supply
  • Fertilizers & nutrients
  • Seeds & planting materials
  • Farming tools & accessories
